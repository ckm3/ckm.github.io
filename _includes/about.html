About

This is Kaiming Cui. I am currently a Ph.D. student in astrophysics at the CAS Key Laboratory of Optical Astronomy, National Astronomical Observatories, Chinese Academy of Sciences (NAOC) and the University of Chinese Academy of Science (UCAS).

Research Interests

  • Relationship between stellar magnetic activity and stellar fundamental parameters (e.g., rotation period, age, and metallicity).

  • Stellar activity and the affects on exoplanets (e.g., flare rate, starspots coverage).

  • Mining data and modeling light curves in photometric data (e.g., Kepler, K2, and TESS).

  • Detection, modeling and pulsation analysis of binary stars.
